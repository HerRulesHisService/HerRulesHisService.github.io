LGBTQ+ Intimacy Checklist & Scene Negotiator

A comprehensive digital tool for exploring and negotiating intimacy interests with partners in a safe, private environment.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Complete Your Checklist: Go through each category and select your preference for each activity using the color-coded options.
  2. Save Your Preferences: Click "Save My Preferences" to store your selections.
  3. Compare with Partner: Have your partner complete their checklist on their own device.
  4. Share and Compare: Ask your partner to download their JSON file and share it with you. Then use the "Import Partner's Preferences" button to load their file and compare with yours.
  5. Negotiate Your Scene: Use the comparison results as a starting point for discussing and planning your intimate encounters.

Remember: This tool is for negotiation purposes only. Always practice informed consent and maintain open communication with your partner(s).
